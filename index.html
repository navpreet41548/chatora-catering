WHY CHOOSE CHATORA

We started Chatora to take you and your guests on a journey of authentic Indian Food that will connect you to your roots and traditions.

In today's market, there is a clear lack of a food joint offering traditional Indian flavours and the dishes we had become accustomed to back home.

Born and raised in Delhi, Chef Dhiren is a culinary master of 25 years. Each one of his dishes is reflective of a commitment to preserving authenticity, identity and to ensuring a stirringly sumptuous culinary adventure.

We offer an extensive collection of innovative & pure vegetarian menus, having a diverse range of astonishing food presentations that add the personal touch for each individual occasion.

From the first time you connect with us, to the delivery, we'll make sure your order is right and ready when you need it.
